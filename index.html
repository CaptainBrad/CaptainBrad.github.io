About Bradley

Previously I worked as a product owner and I really enjoyed working within the digital space. As a product owner I had a lot of exposure to the development process and I became intrigued by the developers work. This ultimately led me to General Assembly and learning to code on their Web Development Immersive course so that I could be involved in the build process. I am also attracted to being able to create and problem solve, which was lacking in my previous role.
I really enjoy developing the user interface as well as building RESTful applications using Node, Express, Mongo and a JavaScript framework such as React.js or AngularJS.
